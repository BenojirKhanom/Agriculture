Rice Farming (Modern)

Category: Grain | Summer | Modern

Step-by-Step:
  1. Prepare wet clay soil
  2. Select certified high-yield seeds
  3. Maintain proper water level (2–5 cm)
  4. Apply nitrogen fertilizer
  5. Harvest after 120 days
Tips:
  • Use pest-resistant varieties
  • Monitor irrigation regularly
Best Practices:
  • Use crop rotation
  • Ensure proper drainage system
Tomato Farming (Organic)

Category: Vegetable | Winter | Organic

Step-by-Step:
  1. Prepare loamy soil with compost
  2. Plant organic seeds
  3. Ensure sunlight exposure
  4. Water regularly
  5. Harvest after 70–80 days
Tips:
  • Use natural fertilizers
  • Check pests early
Best Practices:
  • Apply drip irrigation
  • Use neem oil for pest control
Wheat Farming (Modern)

Category: Grain | Winter | Modern

Step-by-Step:
  1. Prepare well-drained soil
  2. Sow seeds evenly
  3. Apply balanced fertilizer
  4. Maintain irrigation schedule
  5. Harvest after 100 days
Tips:
  • Use certified seeds
  • Avoid over-irrigation
Best Practices:
  • Use mechanized tools
  • Monitor soil nutrients

Pest Control Strategies (IPM)

Integrated pest management for sustainable farming

Prevention

Prevent pest infestation before it occurs.

  • Use disease-resistant seeds
  • Maintain proper field hygiene
  • Practice crop rotation
Biological Control

Use natural enemies to control pests.

  • Introduce beneficial insects (ladybugs)
  • Use natural predators
  • Encourage biodiversity
Organic Remedies

Apply eco-friendly pest control solutions.

  • Neem oil spray
  • Garlic/chili solution
  • Soap water treatment
Monitoring & Early Detection
  • Inspect leaves and crops regularly
  • Identify pest symptoms early
  • Use traps for detection
Effective Treatment
  • Apply treatment immediately after detection
  • Use appropriate organic or biological methods
  • Avoid overuse of chemicals
